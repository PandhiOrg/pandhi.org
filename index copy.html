Open Source Databank

KutchWildlife.com

Kutch's first open access wildlife databank.

Kutch has an abundance of wildlife, and an abundance of opportunities for research. We are providing the first central repository for all information regarding the native wildlife species, and research conducted upon them.

  • Open source / open access (CC-BY)
  • Details on every species occurring in Kutch
  • Species identification tools
  • Searchable database, including research papers listing, species by visual properties, sightings by region, and more
  • Rare sightings and roadkill information
  • Image gallery for all species
  • Secure access for sensitive data
  • Android app for reporting sightings, roadkills, and looking up data quickly in the field (coming soon)
