Documentary

Dragons in the Desert

This film follows the life of three crocodiles over the course of a very fateful year.

The crocodile populations of Kutch face ever increasing challenges for survival. Isolated on a land-locked island, facing worsening climate that reduces access to freshwater, and human program encroaching upon their last bastions.





Journey through the harsh lands of Kutch as we explore small oases where crocodiles carve out their territory. Separated by vast empty lands, these crocodiles are very adept at moving between water holes, and even surviving months on end without water or food.

Experience the hidden life of crocodiles through a different point of view, and see the challenges they face in the desert.


The documentary is current in production, and will be released in early 2020.


Dragons in the Desert will be narrated by Lili Bordan (The Martian, Book Club, Battlestar Galactica: Blood & Chrome).
