Contributions

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the Foundation. Operating on our founding principles was charity and self-sufficiency, the Mahim Pandhi Wildlife Foundation is a fully self sufficient organization. If you would like to contribute on in other ways, please contact us.


Collaboration

What we are looking for are other likeminded people - scientists, naturalists, and even amateur herpetologists - who are interested in collaborating with the DesertCrocs project, and beyond. We welcome you to come join our research projects, field work, and education initiatives.

Contact us for details.
